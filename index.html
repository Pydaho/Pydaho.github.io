About Me

I believe I can make a difference by integrating technology into the government, specifically natural resource fields. I have a proven track record of successful digital transformation initiatives, including the development of mobile apps and Robotic Process Automation (RPA) flows through Microsoft Power Apps and Power Automate. More than once I have taken (or queried) collections of spreadsheets, combined them, cleaned them, and then built a user interface for colleagues to easily interact with that data. In my spare time I have started learning Swift, which is a programming language specific to building iOS apps. My philosophy and passion centers around problem-solving and streamlining processes to save time and effort, making program use more efficient and user-friendly.
