About Me

I am confident that I can contribute significantly by incorporating technology into government operations, particularly in the realm of natural resources. My track record includes successfully leading digital transformation initiatives, such as creating mobile apps and implementing Robotic Process Automation (RPA) using Microsoft Power Apps and Power Automate. In my leisure time, I have embarked on learning Swift, a programming language dedicated to constructing iOS apps. My overarching philosophy and passion revolve around problem-solving and optimizing processes to enhance efficiency, ultimately saving time and effort. My aim is to make program usage more streamlined and user-friendly.
